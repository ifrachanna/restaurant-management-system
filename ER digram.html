📋 Project Description

Project Title: Restaurant Management System

Overview: The Restaurant Management System is a comprehensive web-based application designed to streamline restaurant operations by providing separate interfaces for customers and owners. This system digitizes the entire dining experience from table booking to payment processing, while giving restaurant owners powerful tools to manage their business efficiently.

✨ Key Features:
  • Automate Restaurant Operations: Reduce manual work and paperwork
  • Enhance Customer Experience: Provide seamless digital ordering experience
  • Real-time Management: Enable owners to monitor and control operations instantly
  • Data-driven Decisions: Provide analytics and insights for business growth
  • Error Reduction: Minimize human errors in order taking and billing
🗄️ Database Entities: order, Menu, Payment, Tables, Order details, Customer,User_id
